# Grocery-Mart Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-06-22
**Canonical URL:** https://grocery-mart.example/privacy

Grocery-Mart ("we") operates an ethnic-grocery price-comparison and delivery marketplace.
This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, and the rights you can exercise. It is
written to align with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

## Personal data we collect and why

| Category | Data | Purpose | APP basis |
|----------|------|---------|-----------|
| Identity | name, email, phone | account creation, authentication | APP 3 (collection) |
| Location | delivery address, driver GPS (drivers only, consented, while on an active delivery) | delivery routing + live tracking | APP 3, APP 6 |
| Transactions | orders, wallet balance, payments | order fulfilment + settlement | APP 6 |
| Device | FCM push token, platform | order/delivery notifications | APP 6 |
| Content | product reviews you write | community ratings | APP 6 |

We do **not** sell personal data. Driver location is collected only after explicit in-app consent
and only during an active delivery; it is access-controlled and purged after a short retention window.

## Your rights (APP 12 / 13)

- **Access** — request a machine-readable export of your personal data in-app: Account → Download my data.
- **Correction** — correct inaccurate profile fields in-app: Account → Edit profile.
- **Deletion** — delete your account in-app: Account → Delete Account. We anonymize your personal
  identifiers immediately and revoke your sessions. We retain order and settlement records in
  de-identified form where we are legally required to (financial/tax obligations).

## Retention

Personal data is retained only as long as needed for the purpose collected. Driver GPS fixes,
expired OTP challenges, and old audit records are purged automatically on a schedule. Financial and
settlement records are retained for the period required by law.
